Steps to compare LSA and linear transformation of gene expression data:

1.  Generate training/test datasets:  generate_input.py
2.  Train VAE:  tybalt_1layer_model.py
3.  Encode training datasets and save encodings:  apply_saved_model.py
4.  Generate offset vector:  latent_offset.py
5.  Encode test datasets:  apply_saved_model.py
6.  Apply offset vector:  apply_offset.py
7.  Decode test dataset (only for latent space):  apply_saved_model.py
8.  Evaluate transformation of test datasets:  error_geneExp_reconstruction.py
